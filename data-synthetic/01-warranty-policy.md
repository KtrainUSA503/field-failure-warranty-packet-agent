## 1. Coverage Periods (from in-service date)

| Component group | Coverage period | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Structural frame & subdeck | 60 months | Excludes corrosion from de-icing chemicals |
| Floor slats & bearings | 36 months | Normal wear excluded |
| Hydraulic drive cylinder **and cylinder seals** | **24 months** | Covers manufacturing defects in seals, rod, barrel |
| Control valve & switching block | 24 months | |
| Hydraulic hoses & fittings | 12 months | |
| Consumables (filters, fluid, lubricant) | Not covered | |

The **in-service date** is the date recorded in the Contoso Unit Registry at delivery, not
the purchase-order date.

## 2. Exclusions

A claim is **excluded** (denied) if the failure is caused by any of the following:

- **Hydraulic system contamination** (dirt, water, metal particulate in fluid).
- **Operation above rated load** (GlideFloor 200 rated **25 tons** distributed).
- **Impact or collision damage** to the cylinder, rod, or deck.
- **Unauthorized repair** or use of non-Contoso replacement parts.
- **Fluid not meeting spec** (see parts/spec sheet, ISO VG 46 hydraulic oil).
- Normal wear, cosmetic damage, or failure to perform scheduled maintenance.

> Failure-mode classification (covered defect vs. excluded cause) is a **judgment call** and
> must be supported by the field tech's inspection notes. When the cause is uncertain, the
> claim is marked **"Needs review"** — never auto-approved or auto-denied.

## 3. Required Documentation for a Complete Claim

A claim is **rejected on submission** if any of these nine items is missing:

1. Unit **serial number**
2. Failed **part number**
3. **In-service date** (from registry)
4. **Failure date**
5. **Failure-mode description** (tech narrative)
6. **Photographic evidence** of the failed component
7. **Hour-meter / cycle-count reading** at time of failure
8. **Contamination check** result (fluid condition / clean or contaminated)
9. **Operating load** at time of failure

> Items 1–5, 8, and 9 can usually be reconstructed from the customer email, the tech's chat
> notes, and the registry. Items **6 (photo)** and **7 (hour-meter)** are the two most
> commonly missing — they must be collected in the field, and their absence is the #1 cause
> of first-pass rejections.

## 4. Submission

Complete claims are filed to the **Warranty Claims** SharePoint list. A Contoso warranty
administrator reviews each claim within 3 business days. Incomplete claims are returned to
the submitting tech with a list of missing items.
